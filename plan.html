Goals

Townsville Community Music Centre, abbreviated as TCMC, organizes workshops and events like concert throughout the year from the multitude of genres. So, the primary goal of TCMC is to reccruit new members and increase the invlovement of members in the volunteering and promotional activities like promoting concerts, workshops, volunteering for administration. Also, TCMC has to focus on ticket sales of concerts, rope in more events from the performers and also do photo shoot, media releases for the promotion campaign.
In nut shell, TCMC primarily focusses on the organization of events, workshops, concerts, promotional activities, ticket sales and the like.

Target Audience

As TCMC focusses on musical events from the diverse genres like classical, jazz, ethnic music to name a few, so obviously the target audience are from the different walks of life having variety tastes in music. The target audience can be of the variety age groups from young to old. So, TCMC must emphasis on providing distinctive themes on every events. Keeping in mind the audience type, the site must be developed appealing and attracting people of every age group.

Success Evaluation

The site for TCMC is being developed keeping in mind the goals of TCMC and target audience. So, the success can be determined by asking the questions like: "What is the age group of audience?", "What type of people have applied for membership?", "What is the ratio of ticket sales for different sorts of music?". Suppose, if one particular age group dominates as audience, then it means that some tweaks are needed so as to attract people of other age group also. So, if the answers to the aforementioned questions is not satisfactory, then we need to tweak the website to fix the issue.

Content

Initially, we intend to develop following pages. Some new pages may be added or existing pages may be deleted while proceeding with further design.

home page
home page will be displayed when the site is open. Contains the navigation links to other pages, and some useful information like: short introduction of TCMC, glimpses of some events and further.
events page
This page will list the list of events with information like place of the event, artists performing in the event. For now the events menu does not have any sub-menus but if requirement arises, the sub-menus by the name workshops, and concerts can be added to the events menu.
about us page
This page will describe about TCMC in detail along with the history.
artists page
This page will list all the artists with their description.
membership page
The membership page has the information on how to become membership. There is application form to be filled to become the member.
musos page
This page will contain the details of local ensembles.
