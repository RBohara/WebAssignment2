Stokes Nicholson Big Band

The Stokes-Nicholson Big Band is a not for profit community 22 piece Big Band playing Jazz and Big Band music.

Celtic Fyre

Townsville Citizen’s Band Association is a dynamic organisation consisting of three Brass Bands.

Townsville Brass is the organisation’s premier performing ensemble. This 30-piece Brass Band performs regularly for public events, and also participate in competitions on state and national levels.

Despite being a “training band”, Brolga Brass is a vibrant and active performing ensemble. This group allows players of any age to further develop their skills in a more relaxed environment.

Townsville Brass Youth Band provides an excellent platform for young brass and percussion players to learn and showcase skills associated with banding.

Townsville Concert Band

Do you play clarinet, flute, oboe or bassoon? French horn? Electric bass? Trombone?

Improve your skills in a friendly environment and take part in some major musical events!

This is a local community band who meet once a week for rehearsals, with some serious concerts planned throughout the year. The band plays a range of different styles of concert band music, ranging from classical to musical theatre and modern pop.

Aviva - String Quartet

Four talented musicians combine their skills in a variety of musical styles including fiery Rhumbas, Tangos, Serenades - a mixture of the most divine and swoon-worthy pieces, guaranteed to delight the senses.

Their performances take listeners through a wonderful journey of beautiful melodies and the most romantic music that is guaranteed to leave the senses fulfilled and wanting more.
